STARWAKE PROTOCOL — CARRIER DURABILITY & ROSTER REPAIR

- Increased carrier health and wave scaling.
- Carriers begin with a stored missile reserve rather than zero ammunition.
- Missile production now manufactures a rack per cycle, preventing the carrier from presenting only one or two missiles.
- Close-range launches remain split into 30% defensive, 60% attacking, and 10% reserve roles.
- Fixed the late-wave enemy selector: overlapping thresholds had made tanks unreachable after dodgers unlocked.
- Replaced the threshold chain with weighted archetype selection so all unlocked low-tier enemy classes continue spawning.
