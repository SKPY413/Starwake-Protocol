STARWAKE PROTOCOL - PERSISTENT FIRE SYNTH

- Replaced per-shot oscillator allocation for the player's primary weapon with two persistent, normally silent oscillators.
- Each bullet now retriggers AudioParam envelopes and pitch bends without creating a new source node.
- Persistent fire sources use protected priority metadata and cannot be selected as expendable voice-steal targets.
- Added lifecycle cleanup during audio-engine restart.
- Reduced the semantic shot interval from 28 ms to 20 ms so high fire-rate builds remain audible.
- Added `Persistent fire: ready/not-ready` to exported audio diagnostics.
