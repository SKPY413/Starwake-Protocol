STARWAKE PROTOCOL — POWER AND CROWDING REBALANCE

- Removed permanent offensive end-of-wave rewards that compounded every wave.
- Reduced Research Cache payout and Field Repair strength.
- Added non-stacking Momentum Reserve and Emergency Armor rewards.
- Easy mode now has lower enemy speed, slower spawn growth, slower stat growth,
  later archetype unlocks, and a 14-enemy concurrent cap.
- Added per-difficulty concurrent enemy limits.
- Increased minimum spawn distance, especially on Easy.
- Slightly adjusted Easy economy so paid research remains meaningful.
