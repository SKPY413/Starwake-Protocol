STARWAKE PROTOCOL — TWO DAMAGE TYPES

Player damage is now restricted to exactly two types:

1. WEAPON DAMAGE
- Regular player bullets
- Explosive-round splash
- The red weapon relic / Astral Lens

2. QUANTUM DAMAGE
- Quantum Missiles
- Orbital Field
- Rift upgrades
- The purple Rift relic
- Future damaging Quantum-tree systems

The previous generic Ability damage category has been removed. Legacy ability-tagged damage is mapped to Quantum for save/runtime compatibility. Q-NULL enemies therefore resist every damaging Quantum upgrade and the Rift relic, but not normal fire or the weapon relic.
