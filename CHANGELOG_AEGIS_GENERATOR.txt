STARWAKE PROTOCOL — AEGIS GENERATOR

- Added a late-game Aegis Generator beginning around Wave 35.
- Only one natural Aegis Generator may be active at a time.
- Its large field protects every enemy inside it from player weapon and ability damage while the player remains outside.
- The generator itself follows the same rule: the player must enter the field to damage and destroy it.
- The field becomes more transparent while the player is inside and displays GENERATOR EXPOSED.
- Generator movement holds a medium engagement distance and slowly orbits rather than rushing the player.
- Added a cache-busted game script filename for reliable deployment testing.
