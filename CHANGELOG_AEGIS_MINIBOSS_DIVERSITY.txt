AEGIS / MINI-BOSS DIVERSITY PASS

- Increased Aegis shield radius from 255 to 430 world units.
- Removed bomb mutation from mini-tanks, tanks, brutes, and runners.
- Bomb mutation now belongs to mobile fighter and dodger archetypes.
- Mini-tanks now spawn as one of three readable roles:
  * HEALER: repairs up to seven nearby damaged enemies in pulses.
  * SUPER TANK: much higher health, lower speed, heavy spread fire.
  * SNIPER: maintains long range and fires a single fast, high-damage shot.
- Added role-specific colors, projectile behavior, movement, and overhead labels.
