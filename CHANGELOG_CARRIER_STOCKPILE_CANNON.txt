STARWAKE PROTOCOL — CARRIER STOCKPILE + CANNON PASS

CARRIERS
- Carrier scaling now begins at Wave 20.
- Missile manufacturing starts at one munition every 2.0 seconds.
- Manufacturing cadence improves by 1% per wave after Wave 20, capped at 1.5 seconds.
- Stockpile capacity grows by 3% per wave after Wave 20, capped at 72.
- Missiles remain stored inside the carrier while the player is far away.
- Carrier becomes aggressive inside 540 world units and disengages beyond 680.
- Close-range launches occur in controlled batches to prevent instant projectile floods.
- Missile roles are 30% protection, 60% attack, and 10% orbit/reserve.
- Removed the carrier's long-range plasma cannon to reduce visual clutter.

PLAYER CANNON
- Added Cannon Power, Cannon Cycle, and Cannon Penetration to the red weapon research menu.
- Cannon has independent damage, fire rate, and penetration statistics.
- Cannon fires alongside held primary fire but does not inherit primary/spread upgrades.
- Overdrive weapon-speed pickups accelerate cannon cycling.
- Cannon shells use a clean bright slug with no smoke, recoil, screen shake, or extra particles.
- Penetration upgrades let the shell pass through additional enemies.
