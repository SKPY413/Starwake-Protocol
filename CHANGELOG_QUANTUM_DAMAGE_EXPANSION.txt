QUANTUM DAMAGE EXPANSION

- Split player damage into Weapon, Ability, and Quantum categories.
- Rift Tearer now deals Quantum damage rather than generic relic/ability damage.
- Added late-game Q-NULL enemies beginning at wave 32; they are immune to Quantum damage and visibly marked.
- Conventional autonomous missiles, aura, drones, and relic systems remain Ability damage.
- Added repeatable RIFT INTENSITY and RIFT CASCADE research investments.
- Added separate Quantum damage combat text and run-total tracking.
